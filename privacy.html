FocusCut – Privacy Policy

FocusCut does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
All annotations, highlights, dividers, and sticky notes created by the user are stored locally in the browser using Chrome's storage API. No data is transmitted to any external server.

This extension does not require an account or login, and does not use any analytics or tracking tools.
We are fully committed to user privacy and data security.
